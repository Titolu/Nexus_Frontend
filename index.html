B2B API platform for large enterprises

What we do

Nexus API is a B2B SaaS company that builds and operates APIs for large enterprises. We don't sell to consumers; our customers are companies like Netflix, Facebook, healthcare systems, and investment firms. They integrate our APIs into their products and internal tools.

We are post–Series A, with a small engineering team and a growing list of enterprise deals. We run on AWS, GCP, and Azure.

Product portfolio

Four product lines. Each exposes APIs used by big companies and touches different compliance and risk areas.

1. Payment Solutions API

What it is: APIs that let large firms (e.g. Netflix, Facebook, major retailers) receive and reconcile payments from their customers and partners.

Who uses it: Finance and payments teams at enterprises; high transaction volume.

2. Workplace Connect

What it is: A social/collaboration platform similar to an internal social feed, restricted to a single company's employees. Used for internal comms, culture, and engagement.

Who uses it: HR and internal communications at enterprises.

3. Healthcare Data Management

What it is: APIs and a backend system for healthcare providers and payers to manage patient and claims data: demographics, encounters, diagnoses, and billing-related data.

Who uses it: Hospitals, clinics, insurers, health tech vendors.

4. Blockchain Wallet for Investors

What it is: Custody and wallet APIs for institutional investors to hold and move digital assets. Integrates with existing custody and trading workflows.

Who uses it: Asset managers, family offices, crypto-native funds.
